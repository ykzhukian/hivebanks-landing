The main difference from digital currency exchanges is that digital asset banks do not have the concept of placing orders. Users cannot set a certain price to sell or buy a certain digital currency.

Like ordinary banks that can exchange currency, users can use the CNY to exchange dollars in banks or convert them into CNY.

Because it is impossible to set up fixed-price buy orders and sell orders, people will not call foreign currency exchange banks foreign currency exchanges.

In addition, although people can convert legal currency into digital currency through digital assets bank, or convert digital currency into legal currency. However, unlike digital currency exchanges, digital banks charge a high commission for the customer's redemption operations to curb the impulse of frequent user transactions.

Since digital currencies are mostly issued by deflation, unlike ordinary banks, digital asset banks generally adopt zero or even negative interest rates.

Therefore, digital asset banks may also charge users for the storage of digital assets, and general digital currency exchanges will not charge such fees.

Digital banks do not provide futures or leverage like digital currency exchanges, and are more suitable for conservative investors.

How do digital currency and legal currency of digital assets bank work

After the digital currency agent pays a certain amount of digital currency to the digital asset bank as a security deposit, and obtains digital bank approval, it can provide the users of the digital asset bank with digital currency recharge and withdrawal operations within the margin range.

Digital currency agency agent recharge operation process:
Recharging users according to the recharge rate set by digital currency agents in digital asset banks. Send a certain amount of digital currency to the digital currency payment address specified by the digital currency agent.

After the digital currency agent confirms the payment, it transfers a part of the balance of the agent in the digital asset bank account to the recharge user account and completes a digital currency agent recharging operation.

Digital currency agency agent withdrawal operation process:
The withdrawal is based on the user’s withdrawal rate set by the digital currency agent in the digital asset bank. Transfer a portion of their balance in the digital asset bank account to the digital currency agent’s account.

After the digital currency agent confirms the payment, the designated number of digital currency is sent to the digital currency receiving address specified by the withdrawal user, and a digital currency agent withdrawal operation is completed.

Franc currency agents operate similarly to digital currency agents. The only difference is that the digital currency collection address is replaced with a legal currency collection account.
